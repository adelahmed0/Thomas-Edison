Biography

Thomas Edison, an American inventor, was born in Ohio in 1847. He was described as the man who lit up the world after inventing the electric light bulb on October 21, 1879. He was also known as the "owner of a thousand inventions" for recording about 1,093 patents. To be the fourth most productive inventor in history.
